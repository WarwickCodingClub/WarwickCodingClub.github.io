Share and learn R programming for biologists
Warwick Coding Club is a peer-to-peer learning initiative by two postgraduate students. 
Our Aims
We aim to create a positive, peer-learning environment for developing and sharing programming and statistics skills, using R. 

During term time we will run and organise weekly workshops. 

These workshops will be about an hour and a half, with lots of time for questions and practice.

Wednesday12.30 - 2 MRI room 

These will be open to all levels of experience and all people - undergraduate, postgraduate and researchers.
